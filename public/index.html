📌 Description

Tubeshad helps users improve their English pronunciation 🗣️ and listening skills 🎧 by mimicking native speakers. It integrates speech recognition 🎙️ to compare the original text with the user’s recorded speech and provides feedback.

⭐ Key Features

  • 📺 Video Practice Mode: Watch YouTube videos with transcripts to practice listening & shadowing.
  • 🗣️ Speech Recognition: Compares the user’s speech with the transcript for accuracy.
  • 📖 Vocabulary Learning:
    • 🎴 Flashcard feature selects 10 random words, providing definitions & multiple-choice questions.
    • 🏷️ CEFR level-based word styling (A1, A2, B1, etc.) for better learning.
  • 📝 Quiz Mode: Tests users on vocabulary & pronunciation.
  • 📥 Offline Support: Save videos & words.
